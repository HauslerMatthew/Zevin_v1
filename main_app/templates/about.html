ZEVIN MUSIC

Artist Bio

Kevin Zeiger (a.k.a. ZEVIN) is a guitarist, bassist, pianist, accordionist, vocalist, and songwriter based in the Chicagoland area. In 2014 he debuted his first solo release under the name "Zevin," and has since published four additional releases (listed below).

ZEVIN is an ever-evolving project. There is no 'true' musical direction with ZEVIN, just melodic possibilities. As a fan of many MANY different genres, the aim is to create a vast array of different sounds and translate them to the live stage as fully as possible. Drawing influence from several different areas (not just music) truly helps to craft something unique, so expect the unexpected.
